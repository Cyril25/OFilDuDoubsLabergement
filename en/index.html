Welcome to Alisson and Cyril's - O'Fil du Doubs

Located in the heart of the village of Labergement-Sainte-Marie, our 70 m² accommodation welcomes you in a peaceful and enchanting setting, with an unobstructed view and no overlooking neighbors.

Just a few steps from Lake Remoray, 8 km from the Métabief ski resort, and 17 km from the Swiss border, O'Fil du Doubs is the ideal place to recharge in any season.

Whether you are a fan of hiking, water sports, cycling, or winter sports, you will find here a privileged starting point to explore the natural and cultural wonders of the region.

We will be delighted to welcome you and share our little piece of paradise with you!
