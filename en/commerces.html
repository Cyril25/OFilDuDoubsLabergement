Village Shops

Everything you need is nearby

The village of Labergement-Sainte-Marie offers a variety of shops and services near our accommodation, such as a bakery, a grocery store, a tobacco, press, and lottery shop, and a restaurant. Whether you need to shop or dine, everything is within reach.

La Fruitière des Lacs

Cheese dairy renowned for its local cheeses: Comté, Morbier, Cancoillotte, Mont d’Or... as well as a wide selection of regional products (closed Sunday afternoon and early afternoon).

Butchery and delicatessen with local products such as the famous Morteau sausage (closed Thursday, Sunday afternoon, and midday).
