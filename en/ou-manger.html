Where to eat

Restaurants and food trucks near the accommodation

Fancy a treat without cooking? Check the schedule of food trucks that set up every day in Labergement-Sainte-Marie and the surrounding villages, along with our selection of nearby restaurants.

Restaurants

A selection of good places to eat near the accommodation: from the village bistro to fine dining, not forgetting the cheese specialities of the Haut-Doubs. Remember to book, especially in high season and for the most popular spots.

Food trucks

For each day of the week, find the food trucks present and where they are located. Most set up in the late afternoon and evening. Those in Labergement are within walking distance of the accommodation. Call them to check exact opening hours and the day's menu.
