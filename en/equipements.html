Comfort and Equipment

Everything you need to meet your needs

Our accommodation is fully equipped to meet all your needs. Whether you're here for work or leisure, you'll find all the modern comforts for a pleasant stay.

Wifi

Internet accessible via wifi

Multimedia

116 cm smart TV in the living room and 80 cm TV in the main bedroom

For Baby

Travel cot and high chair available

Electric Shutters

All shutters are electric for your comfort

In the Kitchen

Coffee pods, coffee filters, oil, salt and pepper, tea bags

Linen Provided

Bed linen and towels included in the rental

Consumables

Toilet paper, paper towels, dishwasher tablets, small trash bags, soap, shower gel, and shampoo

Recycling

Bins available for recycling

Parking

Free parking spaces available at the bottom of the residence

Outdoor Equipment

Garden furniture, table and chairs, barbecue

Small Appliances

Toaster, raclette machine, fondue set, Seb Easy Fry fryer, hairdryer, Nespresso coffee machine, kettle, filter coffee maker, iron (with ironing board and drying rack included)

Large Appliances

Oven, microwave, fridge-freezer, induction hob, dishwasher, washing machine

House Rules

  • Parties are strictly prohibited.
  • Smoking is not allowed inside the accommodation, but it is permitted on the balcony.
  • Pets are allowed upon request.

Accessibility

The accommodation is located on the first floor without an elevator. You need to climb 16 steps to access it. A handrail is available to assist those who need support.
